# Executive Summary

## Business Problem

Unexpected vehicle failures increase operational costs, delay deliveries, reduce customer satisfaction, and create safety risks.

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop a predictive maintenance machine learning model that analyzes historical maintenance records together with real-time sensor data.

---

## Expected Benefits

- Reduced downtime
- Lower maintenance costs
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Increased fleet reliability
- Improved driver safety

---

Target model accuracy:

90%
